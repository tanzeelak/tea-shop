Hey there, we're Cha Lounge (aka Tanzeela and Amber).

Living in San Francisco, we're both creatives and entrepeneurs. Rigorous prototyping is at the core of each of our blends. The two of us have uncovered how different teas and flowers come together to create something beautiful.

Tea is more than just a drink. It's intentionality, nourishment, a ritual that embodies the nuance of everyday life. Every blend we share is something we genuinely love.

We hope you will too.
