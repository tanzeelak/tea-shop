For years, we've been brewing, tasting, and blending, curious about how different teas, flowers, and botanicals come together to create something beautiful in a cup.

Living in San Francisco, surrounded by builders and entrepreneurs, experimentation is in the air. In tech people prototype ideas; in our kitchen we prototype teas, testing flavors, refining blends, and following curiosity wherever it leads.

To us, tea is more than just a drink. It's intentionality. It's nourishment. It's a small ritual that adds richness to everyday life.

Every blend we share is something we genuinely love to drink ourselves.

We hope you will too.
